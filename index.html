LUTSK CASTLE

The image of the castle has long been used as a symbol of LUTSK. One of the largest, oldest and best-preserved castles in Ukraine. The tower of Lutsk Castle is depicted on our 200-hryvnia banknote. The castle was included in the list of "Seven Wonders of Ukraine" and is one of the most recognizable historical objects of Volyn region.

Read more
LUTHERAN CHURCH

An architectural monument, an object of the historical and cultural reserve"Old Lutsk". It was built in 1907 in the neo-Gothic style. It became one of the main temples of German colonists on the territory of Volyn. Now it is an architectural monument, which occupies a significant aesthetic place in the general background of the Lutsk Reserve.

HOUSE OF THE SCULPTOR
MYKOLA HOLOVAN

An interesting architectural monument and a unique residential building in the city. It is located on the territory of the historical and cultural reserve "Old Lutsk", on the banks of the Styr River. Modern sculptor Mykola Golovan built a unique house according to the project of Volyn architect Rostyslav Metelnytskyi.
